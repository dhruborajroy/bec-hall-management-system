About

Barisal Engineering College is an undergraduate government engineering college located at Durgapur along the Barisal-Bhola highway in Chandpura Union of Barisal Sadar Upazila, Bangladesh. It is the fourth engineering college in Bangladesh. This educational institution is affiliated to the Faculty of Engineering and Technology of the University of Dhaka and it offers a four-year B.Sc. in Engineering degree.
